Dr. Kusum Gynecology Centre

It was started in the year 2021 by Dr. Kusum Lata Bhardwaj - the best doctor for women laparoscopic treatment in Gurgaon, with the aim of providing affordable services to women across Delhi NCR. It is located in the heart of Golf course Road Gurgaon, Sec 55 Khas and is mainly an outpatient clinic. The Gynecological and laparoscopic procedures are done at our associated Hospitals. Dr Kusum is known best for her laparoscopic surgical skills for various Gyne problems like Endometroisis, fibroid and ovarain cysts. Her compassionate nature and genuine care for patients are evident in her every interaction, embodying kindness and empathy in her practice.

AIIMS( New Delhi)

Dr. Kusum Lata Bhardwaj joined All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi in the year 2012 a Senior Resident. she showed keen interest in the field of gynecology and infertility chose to excel in it. During her days at AIIMS she was closely interested in the field of Laparoscopy and wrote many research papers and publications. She learnt safe surgical skills of laparscopy and many women benefitted from her minimal invasive surgery expertise. Later on she served as a faculty also in AIIMS, During this long and hardworking journey at AIIMS, Dr.Kusum Lata Bhardwaj expertise in laparoscopic surgery and compassionate women care contributed greatly to improving the medical care and outcomes for numerous women, enhanching their quality of life and health.

PARAS HEALTHCARE
